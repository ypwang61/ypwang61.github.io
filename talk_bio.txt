Yiping Wang is a Ph.D. student in the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Washington, advised by Prof. Simon Shaolei Du. He previously studied computer science and mathematics at Zhejiang University. His long-term goal is to develop safe and scalable AI systems with superhuman capabilities that advance scientific progress. His current research focuses on reinforcement learning for reasoning in large language models and AI for mathematics, and he has also explored multimodal learning and machine learning theory. He is a recipient of the Amazon AI Ph.D. Fellowship and has interned at Microsoft.
